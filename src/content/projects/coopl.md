### Overview

**Coopl** is a professional e-commerce website developed to showcase and sell industrial heating solutions, including thermocouples and related products.  
The focus of this project was to build a scalable, SEO-optimized platform that enhances visibility and drives online sales.

### Tech Stack

- **WordPress** — for a flexible and widely supported CMS foundation
- **WooCommerce** — to manage the full e-commerce functionality
- **PHP** — powering the backend with custom logic and integrations
- **SEO Optimization** — ensuring strong search visibility and performance
- _(Optional)_ **Elementor or custom theme** — for modern, responsive page design

### Features

- Full-featured online store with product management via WooCommerce
- SEO-optimized structure and meta configuration for better rankings
- Responsive, mobile-friendly design
- Secure checkout and order management
- Easily maintainable and extendable through WordPress plugins

### Outcome

This project enhanced my expertise in **WordPress development**, **WooCommerce customization**, and **SEO strategy**.  
I created a reliable and professional online store that combines usability, performance, and search visibility — effectively supporting the brand’s digital growth.
